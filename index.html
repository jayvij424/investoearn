Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about mutual fund investments and our services.

You can start investing in mutual funds with as little as ₹500 per month through SIPs (Systematic Investment Plans). For lumpsum investments, the minimum amount varies by fund but typically starts from ₹1,000 to ₹5,000. We believe in making investing accessible to everyone, regardless of budget.

Mutual funds generate returns in two ways: through capital appreciation (when the value of the securities in the fund increases) and through dividends or interest earned on those securities. These returns can either be reinvested (growth option) or distributed to investors (dividend option). Over the long term, equity mutual funds have historically outperformed traditional instruments like fixed deposits, making them an attractive option for wealth creation.

All investments carry some level of risk. Mutual funds are relatively safer than direct stock investments as they offer diversification and professional management. However, they are subject to market risks. The level of risk varies by fund type - equity funds have higher risk but potentially higher returns, while debt funds are generally less volatile. We help you select funds based on your risk tolerance, goals, and investment horizon to create a balanced portfolio.

The tax treatment depends on the type of fund and holding period:

  • Equity Funds: Short-term capital gains (held < 1 year) are taxed at 15%, while long-term gains (> 1 year) above ₹1 lakh per year are taxed at 10% without indexation.
  • Debt Funds: Short-term gains (< 3 years) are added to your income and taxed as per your slab, while long-term gains are taxed at 20% with indexation benefits.
  • ELSS Funds: These equity funds offer tax deduction under Section 80C up to ₹1.5 lakhs with a lock-in period of 3 years.

We can help you structure your investments in a tax-efficient manner.

We operate on a commission-based model, where we receive a portion of the expense ratio from the fund houses for the investments made through us. This means there are no direct charges to you as an investor. We are committed to complete transparency and will always disclose the commission structures for any fund we recommend. Our focus is on providing unbiased advice that aligns with your best interests, not on pushing products with the highest commissions.
